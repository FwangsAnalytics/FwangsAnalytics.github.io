About Me

I’m a Data Analyst passionate about transforming raw data into actionable insights. With strong skills in Power BI, Excel, SQL, and Python, I design interactive dashboards and automated reporting systems that help organizations make smarter decisions. I currently serve as Team Lead, Research at the Plateau State Peace Building Agency, where I build data systems to track conflict, resettlement, and peacebuilding activities across 17 LGAs.

Conflict Incident Dashboard – PPBA

An interactive Power BI dashboard tracking conflict incidents across 17 LGAs of Plateau State. It helps leadership identify hotspots and trends for evidence-based decision-making.

Activity Monitoring Dashboard – PPBA

A performance dashboard integrating data from Excel and SharePoint to monitor PPBA’s interventions. It provides a single source of truth for progress tracking and accountability.

Business Profit & Sales Dashboard

A Power BI solution built for a small business to track sales, expenses, and profit margins. It improves financial planning and decision-making through dynamic KPIs.

Personal Finance & Budget Analyzer

An Excel-based tool using PivotTables and Power Query to track income, expenses, and savings goals. It automates reports and provides insights into financial discipline.
